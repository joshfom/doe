Prospecting.

Prospecting with AI agentic capabilities

An AI agent reaches out to wealthy investors most likely to buy into Bayn — many of whom don't yet know it exists for them. It studies a unit, finds the right people, and writes each a personalized approach, with your team in full control.

Why prospecting?

Demand we never see

The best buyers for premium Bayn clusters rarely raise their hand. Many ultra-high-net-worth individuals simply don't know a project like this exists for them — so we go to them first.

Follow the funding train

Track fresh liquidity — fundraises, exits, IPOs — and reach new money while it's actively looking for somewhere to land. The earlier the touch, the warmer the deal.

Create pipeline, don't wait

Stop depending on inbound. Proactively put the right unit in front of the right investor — turning quiet, high-value clusters into active conversations.

How it works
1

Pick the unit

Select Bayn → a cluster (e.g. Views 3), then set price range, unit type & bedrooms — the DNA that defines who buys and what to compare it to.

Bayn → Cluster
2

Ground in the market

Instantly pulls real comparable sales, price/sqft, velocity and the area price trend (YoY, yield). Every figure is sourced — never invented.

Property Monitor · DLD
3

Infer the buyer

From who actually bought comparable units, the AI proposes the investor profile — segments, feeder markets, titles. The rep can edit it.

AI buyer hypothesis
4

Find real prospects

Searches investor databases over API for named people matching the profile — title, company, country — as a shortlist to curate.

Apollo · Crunchbase
5

AI writes the outreach

One click drafts a personalized Email, WhatsApp or call script (EN/AR) fusing the unit, the prospect & real comps. Rep edits, approves, sends.

Channel-aware · bilingual
6

Promote to Lead

An engaged prospect is promoted into a Lead and flows into Salesforce — owner assignment, routing, one reportable funnel.
