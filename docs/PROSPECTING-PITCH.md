# DOE · New Module: **Prospecting**
### C-Level Buy-In Pitch
*AI-assisted outbound for Bayn — from a unit to a qualified, contacted investor in minutes.*

> Format: ~14 slides. Each slide = headline + on-slide bullets + speaker notes. Designed for a 12–15 minute executive pitch with a 3-minute live demo.

---

## Slide 1 — Title

**DOE Prospecting**
Turn any Bayn unit into a pipeline of the right investors — researched, matched, and contacted by an AI agent, with your team in control.

*Speaker note:* "Today DOE handles inbound leads. This module adds the other half — proactive, outbound prospecting — and it does the heavy lifting with AI."

---

## Slide 2 — The opportunity we're missing

- We market Bayn and **wait for inbound leads**. The best buyers for premium clusters rarely raise their hand first.
- Finding and approaching the right investor today is **manual, slow, and inconsistent** — research, market-checking, and writing every message by hand.
- Result: reps work a handful of prospects a day, message quality varies, and high-value clusters get under-sold.

*Speaker note:* "We're leaving the highest-intent, highest-value buyers on the table because outbound is too expensive in human hours."

---

## Slide 3 — The big idea (one line)

> **Pick a Bayn unit → the AI tells you who the likely buyers are, finds real ones, and writes each a personalized, market-backed message — in minutes, not hours.**

- It works the way a top broker would, but at machine speed and consistency.
- Your team stays in full control: **the AI drafts, the human approves and sends.**

---

## Slide 4 — Where it lives

- A **new workspace inside DOE** — no new system to learn, no new login.
- Sits alongside the existing Lead Engine: a prospect that responds **promotes straight into a Lead** and flows into **Salesforce** like every other deal.
- One continuous journey: **Prospect → Outreach → Lead → Salesforce → Close.**

*Speaker note:* "This isn't a bolt-on tool. It's an extension of the pipeline we already run."

---

## Slide 5 — Step 1: Start from what we're selling (Bayn → Cluster)

- The rep selects **Bayn**, then the specific **cluster** (e.g. *Views 3 Villas*, *Marina Resort Suites*, *Hillside Villas*).
- They confirm three things about the unit:
  - **Price range** (e.g. AED 6–15M)
  - **Unit type** (villa / apartment / penthouse)
  - **Number of bedrooms**

**Why these three?** They are the DNA of a property. They determine *who* buys it and *what* it should be compared against. A 4-bed villa at AED 12M and a 1-bed apartment at AED 1.5M attract completely different buyers — these inputs let the AI be precise instead of generic.

*Speaker note:* "Thirty seconds of input. Everything downstream is driven by it."

---

## Slide 6 — Step 2: Ground it in real market data

- The system instantly pulls **comparable sales** around that cluster: recent prices, price per sqft, sales velocity, and the **area price trend** (YoY growth, gross yield).
- Every figure is **real and sourced** — never invented by the AI.

**Data source — our recommendation:**
| Need | Source | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Transactions & price index | **Property Monitor** (primary) | Broadest, cleanest UAE coverage via API; reseller-grade, ready now |
| Official record (phase 2) | **DLD / Dubai Pulse** | Authoritative "source of record" once API access is provisioned |

- The architecture is **source-agnostic** — we can swap or add a source without rebuilding anything.

*Speaker note:* "This is the credibility layer. Our outreach speaks with today's market facts, like an analyst wrote it."

---

## Slide 7 — Step 3: The AI proposes the buyer profile

- Based on **who actually bought comparable units**, the AI builds a buyer hypothesis:
  - Segments (family offices, HNW individuals, golden-visa holders, international investors)
  - Feeder markets and likely titles/seniority
- The rep can **edit the profile** before searching.

*Speaker note:* "We're not guessing the buyer — we're inferring it from real transaction behaviour, then letting a human sanity-check it."

---

## Slide 8 — Step 4: Find real prospects (over API)

- The agent searches **investor & contact databases** for real people matching the profile:
  - **Apollo** — people & company search, titles, seniority, contactability
  - **Crunchbase** — founders, funding events, liquidity signals (recently-exited investors)
- Returns named candidates — title, company, country — as a shortlist the rep curates.

*Speaker note:* "Potential-investor data is available on tap via API. The agent does the searching; the rep picks who's worth pursuing."

---

## Slide 9 — Step 5: The AI writes the outreach (the headline feature)

- For any chosen prospect, **one click** drafts a fully personalized message that fuses:
  1. **The property** (Bayn cluster, price, beds, type)
  2. **The prospect** (their profile and wealth signals)
  3. **Comparable sales** (real figures, cited as proof)
- Choose the **channel** — Email, WhatsApp, or **Phone Call Script** — and **language (EN / AR)**.
- The AI drafts first; the **rep edits, approves, and sends**. Nothing auto-sends, and every number traces to a real record.

*Speaker note:* "This is what turns an hour of work into two minutes. Show the live draft here."

---

## Slide 10 — Step 6: Promote to Lead → continue in Salesforce

- A prospect who engages is **promoted to a Lead** in one click.
- It enters the **existing pipeline**: owner assignment, routing, and **Salesforce sync** — no re-entry, no lost context.
- Outbound and inbound converge into **one reportable funnel.**

*Speaker note:* "The prospecting module hands off cleanly to where deals actually get closed."

---

## Slide 11 — Use case walkthrough: *Selling Bayn · Views 3 Villas*

1. **Input (30s):** Rep picks Bayn → *Views 3*, 4-bed villa, AED 6–15M.
2. **Market (instant):** AI shows comps — a comparable 4-bed sold at AED 12M (~AED 4,500/sqft); area up 4.2% YoY, ~7.4% yield.
3. **Profile (instant):** AI proposes "family offices & HNW investors, India/UK/GCC feeders." Rep approves.
4. **Prospects (seconds):** AI returns named candidates from Apollo/Crunchbase — e.g. a founder post-liquidity in Mumbai, a family-office principal in Riyadh.
5. **Outreach (1 click):** AI drafts a personalized email citing the real comp + yield, in the rep's voice. Rep tweaks one line, approves, sends.
6. **Pipeline:** Prospect replies → promoted to Lead → working it in Salesforce.

**Total: minutes, for a prospect that used to take an afternoon.**

## Slide 12 — Why this wins (business value)

- **Speed:** ~1 hour → ~2 minutes per qualified, contacted prospect.
- **Scale:** Each rep works **many more** high-value prospects per day.
- **Consistency:** Every rep performs like the best closer — personalized, on-brand, data-backed.
- **Credibility:** Outreach leads with current market proof — it reads as advice, not spam.
- **Bilingual:** Native EN/AR for the local market.

*Speaker note:* "The ROI is straightforward: more qualified conversations per rep, at higher quality, on our highest-margin clusters."

---

## Slide 13 — Built-in trust & control (de-risking)

- **Human-in-the-loop:** AI drafts; a person approves every send.
- **No fabricated facts:** every figure is pinned to a real source record.
- **Provenance & compliance:** sources stamped; outbound consent and opt-out respected.
- **Source-agnostic & vendor-safe:** swap Property Monitor / DLD / Apollo without re-engineering.
- **Already integrated:** lives in DOE, hands off to Salesforce.

*Speaker note:* "This is the answer to 'can we trust the AI?' — it proposes, we dispose, and everything is traceable."
